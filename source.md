Standing majestically next to the temple is the 24-foot tall **Kanyakumari Sri Jaya Hanuman**, symbolizing the 24,000 slokas of the Valmiki Ramayana. The temple is a hub of regular activity, celebrating numerous festivals such as Vaikunta Ekadasi and Vasanta Panchami, and performing **daily *annadhaanam*** (free food distribution) to those in need under the Sri Sridhar Ayyaval Annadaana Scheme.
